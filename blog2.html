Why Board Games Are So Popular

July 4, 2022


Board games are getting more popular by the day. The global board games market is worth over $7.2 billion and
is set to rise to $12 billion by 2023 according to board games market value data on Statistica.


The Internet

Board games are getting popular for many reasons. The internet has expanded its visibility to many different
people of different ages. With this, more people are developing and making games each year.


Tabletop Games are Popular on Youtube

Tabletop games have their own community now, especially on youtube. Enthusiasts can sit and watch different board
games for hours on end with the amount of content that has been put out. Furthermore, youtube is home to a variety
of tournaments that people can watch live or pre-recorded.


The Internet

The internet is making it so much easier for people to share their passion for tabletop games. There are many
websites that encourage people to have discussions, one of them being reddit. Reddit is home to many different
topics and communities, and so it encourages these people to post updates or tell stories about their passions.


Podcasts on Board Games

Board games have podcasts now, which is a good way to educate and spread awareness on this hobby.
