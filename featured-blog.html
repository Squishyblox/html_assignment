A Quick Guide to Tabletop Games

July 4, 2022


What is Tabletop Gaming?

Tabletop gaming is a term widely used to represent physical board games/card games. Some examples of simple board
games are Uno, Monopoly, Dominoes, Pictionary, Upwords, Snakes and Ladders, and so on. There are also a group of
more intricate board games like Settlers of Catan, Battlestar Galactica, Magic the Gathering, and so on. Some
people go for the easier ones, and the more hardcore players prefer the harder games that take longer and more
brainpower.



Why Play Games?

For many adults, gaming is viewed as a children’s pastime, and may be frowned upon. However, there are many
benefits to playing games. They’re entertaining and they keep your mind active as you must think a lot in certain
games. Moreover, it can be used to spend time with friends and family.


How do I Learn A Game?

Sometimes a good way is to play the game with some experts, and usually you should be able to catch up. Some
people also prefer reading the booklet that comes with every board game. It will certainly help you get started
with the main components and slowly you’ll get the hang of the entire game. Alternatively, you can watch youtube
videos on how to play the game or watch reviews to see if you will like the game.
